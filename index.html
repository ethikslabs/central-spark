01

Gestalt
Therapy

What is Gestalt Therapy?

A humanistic psychotherapy approach that emphasises personal responsibility and focuses on the present moment. It helps individuals gain awareness of their thoughts, feelings, and actions to achieve personal growth and self-acceptance.

Key concepts

  • Present moment — becoming aware of current thoughts, feelings, and behaviours to foster greater self-awareness and insight.
  • Wholistic approach — recognises the interconnectedness of mind and body, addressing emotional, cognitive, and physical aspects of experience.
  • Integration — clients are encouraged to integrate fragmented parts of self and experience a more cohesive sense of identity.
  • Experiential techniques — including Parts Work and the Empty Chair technique, facilitating emotional expression and resolution.

Benefits

  • Enhanced self-awareness and personal insight
  • Improved relationships and communication
  • Personal growth and lasting transformation

What is Applied Mindfulness?

The practice of being fully present in the moment, paying attention to your thoughts, feelings, and bodily sensations without judgment. By practising mindfulness, you cultivate calm, awareness, and focus.

Techniques including meditation, deep breathing, and body scans help reduce stress, improve mental clarity, and enhance emotional wellbeing. Careena integrates mindfulness practically and intentionally — not as a separate add-on, but as a living, embodied skill.

03

EMDR
Therapy

What is EMDR?

Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing (EMDR) is a highly effective approach designed to help individuals process and heal from traumatic experiences. Widely used to treat PTSD and other conditions including anxiety, depression, and phobias.

Conditions EMDR addresses

  • Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)
  • Anxiety disorders and panic attacks
  • Depression
  • Phobias
  • Relational wounds and attachment injuries
  • Shame-based memories and core negative beliefs

04

Depth
Enquiry™

What is Depth Enquiry™?

An innovative therapeutic method designed to resolve trauma and underlying shame. Developed by Amanda Gruhn, it emerged from extensive work with clients experiencing PTSD and trauma-related symptoms.

Built upon the principles of EMDR and informed by somatic theories from Bessel van der Kolk, Pat Ogden, and Peter Levine — grounded in Gestalt psychotherapy. Careena was among the first therapists in Australia trained in Depth Enquiry™.

Why Depth Enquiry™?

  • Effective resolution of resistant traumas and negative beliefs
  • Gentle, supportive process ensuring a safe therapeutic environment
  • Holistic — combines EMDR with somatic theory and Gestalt psychotherapy

What is EMDR?

EMDR is a focused therapy that helps the brain reprocess distressing experiences so they no longer feel immediate, overwhelming, or stuck. When difficult memories are properly integrated, they lose their emotional charge and stop intruding into the present.

I offer EMDR for adults who want careful, structured work on distressing or overwhelming experiences — whether recent or long-standing — including trauma, relational wounds, phobias, performance blocks, medical events, shame-based memories, or attachment injuries that feel unresolved despite insight and effort.

Sessions are paced thoughtfully and grounded in a steady therapeutic relationship, so that processing feels contained rather than destabilising.

What to expect

The EMDR process

01

Preparation

We take time to understand your history, identify specific memories or triggers, and build strategies to ensure you feel steady and resourced before processing begins.

02

Processing

We focus on one memory or theme at a time, using bilateral stimulation (such as eye movements) to help the brain reprocess the experience. You remain aware and in control throughout. The aim is not to re-live events, but to allow them to become integrated.

03

Integration

Sessions are paced to your capacity and we adjust as needed. EMDR can be brief and targeted, or integrated into longer-term therapeutic work, depending on your goals.

EMDR is most effective when carefully prepared and thoughtfully delivered. If you'd like to explore whether it's appropriate for you, we can discuss this together.

Feeling anxious about going to therapy is a very common experience, especially if it's your first time or if you've had previous negative experiences. This anxiety often arises from the fear of the unknown, vulnerability, or concerns about discussing personal issues with a stranger. Therapy is a safe space designed to help you explore these feelings at your own pace. By discussing your anxiety with Careena, you can work together to make the process more comfortable and tailored to your needs.
The duration of therapy varies widely depending on individual needs, goals, and the type of therapy you're receiving. Some people find significant relief in just a few sessions, while others may benefit from longer-term therapy spanning months or even years. Factors such as the complexity of the issues, your progress, and your commitment to the process will influence the timeline. Careena will provide guidance based on her experience and your specific goals.
It's common to worry about becoming dependent on your therapist. While it's important to build trust with Careena, the goal is to empower you to develop the tools and confidence to manage life's challenges independently. If you feel overly reliant, discussing these feelings openly with her can help balance the relationship and ensure your therapy remains effective.
Start by asking: what specific issues or challenges am I facing that I want to address? Consider whether you're open to exploring your thoughts and emotions, and whether you're ready to commit time and effort to the process. Reflect on your expectations — what do you hope to achieve? Finally, think about the kind of therapist you'd feel comfortable with, considering their approach, experience, and personality.
Careena is highly trained and experienced in several modalities, including: EMDR Therapy, Gestalt Therapy, Depth Enquiry™, Applied Mindfulness, Psychodynamic Therapy, Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT), Somatic Approaches, and Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT). These diverse methods ensure your therapy experience is both effective and uniquely tailored to you.
Expect to be welcomed. Careena will encourage you to talk as little or as much as you want — the most important thing is that you feel comfortable and that this is your space. It is normal to feel nervous, but this is a safe space designed to help you explore at your own pace.
